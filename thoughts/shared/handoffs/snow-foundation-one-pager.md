# A Live Portfolio Intelligence and Community Evidence View for Snow Foundation

Date: 2026-04-12
Status: Draft for Snow-facing use after final review

## Why this is relevant to Snow

Snow Foundation's public strategy already points toward the exact problem this combined stack solves:

- place-based capital
- social justice and systems change
- community-led work
- stronger shared intelligence
- First Nations-led rheumatic heart disease work
- better visibility of how funding, partnership, and lived outcomes connect

The question is not whether Snow needs another dashboard.

The question is whether Snow would benefit from seeing its own portfolio more clearly in one place:

- where capital is moving
- which themes and places are concentrating
- who the connected actors are
- what the work sounds like in lived experience

## Why this is different

This is not:

- a grant CRM
- a generic impact dashboard
- a storytelling app on the side

It is one evidence chain:

- strategy
- funding surface
- recipient network
- geography
- project evidence
- community narrative

That creates a much better decision object for funders than any one of those layers on its own.
