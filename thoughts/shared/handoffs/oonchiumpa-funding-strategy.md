## Strategic Principles (from the meeting)

1. **Don't chase grants for grants' sake** — every application must serve the 10-year vision for the kids
2. **Shared services model** — treat all projects as different "brands" sharing admin, finance, communications. Reduces risk when one project is quiet.
3. **Talk about one thing at a time** — each funder sees a focused, specific project. Behind the scenes, everything's linked.
4. **Say no to unsustainable commitments** — $100K on people disappears fast. Don't employ people you can't keep.
5. **Own things that generate revenue** — every $ spent on assets (containers, beds, equipment) is an investment that keeps producing. Every $ spent on salaries is gone.
6. **Compound towards bigger funding** — each small activation increases credibility for the $1M+ rounds in future.
7. **Strategic salary allocation** — if Snow pays salaries that NIAA was already covering, that frees NIAA $ for infrastructure. Legal and common with trust-based philanthropy.
