## Recommended first email

Subject:

`A live portfolio intelligence and community evidence view for Snow Foundation`

Body:

Hi Snow team,

We have been looking closely at how Snow publicly describes its work across place-based funding, social justice, sector capacity, and First Nations-led rheumatic heart disease work.

Using CivicGraph / GrantScope together with Empathy Ledger, we assembled a Snow-specific working view that brings together:

- your current public grant and program surface
- a live grantee and geography network
- portfolio signals across ACT, NSW South Coast, Sydney, and broader-country work
- community evidence and transcript-based analysis from Deadly Hearts Trek
- a bridge between grant intelligence, project evidence, and reporting

What feels useful is that this is not a generic dashboard. It is a way of making Snow’s own portfolio more legible in one place: where capital is moving, how themes cluster, what the place layer looks like, and what the work sounds like from community.

We would value 30 minutes to walk you through the working version and get your reaction.

If useful ahead of that, we can send a single public preview route so you can see the shape of the working view before the session.

If useful, we can focus the session on either:

1. RHD and social determinants
2. ACT / NSW South Coast place-based funding

Best,

Ben Knight
