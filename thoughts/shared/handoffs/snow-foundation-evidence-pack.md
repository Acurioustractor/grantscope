## Evidence card 2

### Theme

Story, knowledge, and data sovereignty

### Verified source

Transcript: `Aunty Diganbal May Rose - First Interview`

> "So every experience and every story must be respected because that's their story."

## Evidence card 3

### Theme

Infrastructure and system conditions matter, not awareness alone

### Verified source

Transcript: `Dr Boe Remenyi and Cissy Johns`

### Why it matters

This is the clearest quote set connecting RHD outcomes to broader social determinants and practical system design.

### Quotes

> "my mission is to highlight if I can fall through the gaps, how many others are falling through the gaps"

> "it's great to say you should be washing your sheets every week. But if you don't have a washing machine or a community water filtration system, that's not gonna break"

> "education and awareness is great, but you need to match stuff with something that actually enables people to change"

### Interpretation

This is the strongest transcript-backed justification for presenting Snow's RHD work as a portfolio problem, not only a health message. It links health, infrastructure, justice, and material conditions.

### Suggested use in demo

Use when closing the Empathy Ledger section and moving back to why GrantScope's funding, place, and partner layers matter.
