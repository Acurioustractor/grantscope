## Subject

Research data access — NSW political donation disclosures

## Body

Hello,

I'm writing on behalf of CivicGraph (https://civicgraph.com.au), a civic
infrastructure project resolving public Australian entities across AusTender,
AEC, ACNC, GrantConnect, ABR, and the ATO tax transparency registers. We
publish investigative research into Australian accountability — examples at
https://civicgraph.com.au/reports.

**Request:** a bulk export of NSW political donation disclosures from 2019 to
present. We're particularly interested in:
- Donor name, donor ABN (where declared), amount, recipient party/entity,
  financial year
- Third-party campaigner returns
- Associated entity returns
- Property developer prohibited donor records

Ideal format: CSV, JSON, or XLSX.

**Purpose:** we link state-level donations to federal procurement and
foundation giving data to surface cross-system patterns (see our
"Consulting Class" and "Donor-Contractors" reports). Your data would let us
extend those investigations to the state level.

**How we'd use it:**
- Attributed appropriately in any publication
- Published under open attribution license if permitted
- Cross-referenced with federal data in the CivicGraph atlas
- Not resold or used for commercial targeting

If a direct data export isn't possible, I'd appreciate guidance on the
appropriate path — whether GIPAA formal application, NSW Open Data portal
scheduling, or contacting another unit.

Happy to share our data handling practices, previous outputs, or chat by
phone.

Ben Knight
A Curious Tractor / CivicGraph
