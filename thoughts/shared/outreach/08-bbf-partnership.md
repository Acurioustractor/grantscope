## Subject

Partnership inquiry — CivicGraph × Black Business Finder

## Body

Hello,

I'm writing on behalf of CivicGraph (https://civicgraph.com.au), an open
accountability atlas of Australian public data. We're a project under A
Curious Tractor — civic infrastructure for communities, journalists, and
researchers.

We currently have ~6,200 Supply Nation-registered Indigenous businesses
linked in our graph, cross-referenced against government procurement
contracts to surface who's winning Indigenous Procurement Policy (IPP)
allocated work. What we'd value from Black Business Finder is additional
coverage — businesses you list who aren't Supply Nation registered, so we
can make our Indigenous business mapping more complete.

**What we're asking:**
- Access to your business directory listings (a CSV or API endpoint)
- We'd return the favour with any of: citation/backlink in our reports,
  enriched context on each business (government contract history, funder
  history, board composition), OR support building your own data export
  infrastructure

**Non-commercial use:** data would only be used in research/journalism
contexts. Not resold. Attributed to BBF where surfaced.

**Governance:** any Indigenous-facing publication we produce passes
advisory review before release (see our partner principles at
https://civicgraph.com.au/about/curious-tractor).

Happy to jump on a call to explain more.

Ben Knight
A Curious Tractor / CivicGraph
ben@benjamink.com.au
