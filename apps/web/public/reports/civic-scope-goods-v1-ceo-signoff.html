Slide 2 / Why Goods Needs It

Goods is no longer just a product pilot.

The work now has demand, capital pathways, funder relationships, buyer routes, asset feedback, and community ownership questions moving at the same time.

Demand

High-need NT and QLD communities need beds, washers, fridges, and delivery pathways.

Capital

Snow, QBE, Minderoo, PRF, ACF, and others need different asks and evidence.

Procurement

Remote housing, health, schools, councils, RAP buyers, and platform routes need discipline.

Proof

Asset lifecycle, community stories, and product feedback need to inform the next move.

Relationships

GHL needs to carry clean targets, status, ownership, and follow-up history.

Execution

QBE Program and Actions need a clear queue, not another research dump.

Speaker note The risk V1 solves is scattered operating memory. The system turns live context into ranked action.

Slide 3 / How It Works

One evidence chain becomes three working views.

Inputs

CivicGraph, Goods wiki, assets, foundations, grants, GHL, Goods v2.

Rank

Community need, buyer plausibility, capital fit, partner gaps.

Brief

CEO dashboard compiles the decision brief and live lanes.

Act

Goods Workspace exports selected targets and weekly snapshots.

Run

QBE Program, Actions, and GHL carry execution and follow-up.

Speaker note Civic Scope finds and ranks. Goods v2 runs the action. GHL records the relationship. Asset and procurement signals update the next decision.

Slide 5 / What The Team Can Do Today

The team can move from need to outreach.

Open /goods-workspace. Start from highest-need communities, identify buyer gaps, rank buyers and funders, export targets, push selected outreach into GHL, then work the actions in QBE.

Need-led mode

Start with NT-only community pressure, demand counts, buyer gaps, and mapped signals.

Buyer-led mode

Rank plausible buyers by relationship, role, coverage, contract history, and next action.

Capital-led mode

Match funders, grants, and catalytic capital to stage fit and ask shape.

Outreach handoff

Export CSVs, create weekly snapshots, and push selected targets into GHL.

Speaker note This is not generic grant search. It is the Goods operating motion: need signal to buyer pipeline to capital stack to managed outreach.
